Day 1 - Historical Heritage

09:00 AM

Charminar

Start your day at the iconic symbol of Hyderabad. Built in 1591, this architectural marvel offers stunning views of the old city.

11:30 AM

Mecca Masjid

Visit one of India's largest mosques, located just steps away from Charminar.

01:00 PM

Lunch at Paradise

Enjoy the world-famous Hyderabadi Biryani at Paradise Restaurant.

03:00 PM

Laad Bazaar

Shop for traditional bangles, pearls, and artifacts in this historic market.

Day 2 - Modern Hyderabad

10:00 AM

Ramoji Film City

Visit the world's largest film studio complex and enjoy various attractions.

02:00 PM

HITEC City

Explore the IT hub of Hyderabad and visit modern shopping malls.

05:00 PM

Tank Bund

Evening walk along the Hussain Sagar Lake and see the Buddha statue.

Day 3 - Cultural Experience

09:00 AM

Golconda Fort

Explore the majestic fort known for its acoustic effects and architecture.

02:00 PM

Salar Jung Museum

Visit one of the largest one-man collections of antiques in the world.

06:00 PM

Night Food Tour

Experience the famous street food of Hyderabad at Gachibowli.

Travel Tips

Food

Don't miss the authentic Hyderabadi Biryani, Haleem, and Irani Chai.

Transportation

Use the Metro for convenient travel. Auto-rickshaws are good for short distances.

Shopping

Bargain at local markets. Pearls and bangles make great souvenirs.
