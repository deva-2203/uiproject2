Travel Tips

Food

Try Kerala fish curry, appam, and fresh seafood. Don't miss the traditional Kerala sadhya.

Transportation

Use ferries for scenic travel, auto-rickshaws for short distances, and buses for longer routes.

Shopping

Buy spices, handloom textiles, and traditional handicrafts from local markets.
