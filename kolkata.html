Travel Tips

Food

Try Bengali sweets, street food at Park Street, and the famous Kolkata biryani.

Transportation

Use Metro for quick travel, iconic yellow taxis, and trams for a heritage experience.

Shopping

Visit New Market for variety, College Street for books, and Gariahat for sarees.
